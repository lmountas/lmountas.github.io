About

My name is Lamia Mountassir and I am currently a student at George Mason University studying English with a concentration in writing & rhetoric.
By following this path, I have learned to write and create material for various audiences and situations. For example, I have written and done
research to create content such as how-to videos and white papers. More specifically, I created a how-to video on how Mason students can create
an email signature as well as a white paper on how health experts and scientists on Youtube can address concerns regarding COVID to the public.

During the Spring 2021 semester, I have had the opportunity to work on a collaboration project between GMU and Calvert County, MD, called
Calvert County: Agriculture Past and Present. Throughout this project, I had the opportunity to engage with a farmer about the rural community,
her experiences as a woman in agriculture, and environmental policies. Furthermore, I also learned how to interview and conducted a
1-hour phone interview as well as created a transcript, podcast, and log to supplement the interview. Before I graduate in December of 2021,
I'm currently seeking to begin gaining work experience through opportunities in writing professionally within editorial, journalism,
and digital media internships.
