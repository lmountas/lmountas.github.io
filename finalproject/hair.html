Hair

Hair Color

Auburn

noun
a reddish-brown color

Bleached

adj.
made white or much lighter by a chemical process or by exposure to sunlight

Blond(e)

adj.
(of hair) fair or pale yellow

Brunette/Brunet

adj.
having dark brown hair (typically used of a woman)

Burgundy

adj.
a deep red color like that of burgundy wine

Caramel

adj.
the light brown color of caramel

Copper

noun
a reddish-brown color like that of copper

Dyed

adj.
having been colored with a dye

Ginger

adj.
(chiefly of hair or fur) of a light reddish-yellow or orange-brown color

Highlights

adj.
(usually highlights) a bright tint in the hair, especially one produced by bleaching or dyeing

Jet

noun
a glossy black color

Strawberry Blonde

adj.
denoting hair of a light reddish-blond color

Hair Textures & Types

Coarse

adj.
rough or loose in texture or grain

Curly

adj.
made, growing, or arranged in curls or curves

Fine

adj.
(of a thread, filament, or person's hair) thin

Silky

adj.
of or resembling silk, especially in being soft, fine, and lustrous

Straight

adj.
(of hair) not curly or wavy

Thick

adj.
made up of a large number of things or people close together

Wavy

adj.
having a form or edge that smoothly curves in and out

Hairstyles

Afro

noun
a hairstyle in which naturally kinky or tightly curled hair is styled in such a way as to stand out all around the head

Bangs

noun
a fringe of hair cut straight across the forehead

Beehive

noun
a woman's domed and lacquered hairstyle popular in the 1960s

Bob

noun
(of hair) cut in a bob: she wore a beret over her bobbed blond hair.

Bun

noun
a hairstyle in which the hair is drawn back into a tight coil at the back of the head

Chignon

noun
a knot or coil of hair arranged on the back of a woman's head

Comb Over

noun
hair that is combed over a bald spot in an attempt to cover it

Cornrows

noun
a style of braiding and plaiting the hair in narrow strips to form geometric patterns on the scalp

Crew cut

noun
haircut that is trimmed very close to the head, in particular one in which the hair is shaved at the back and sides and left slightly longer at the front and top

Cropped

adj.
(especially of hair) cut very short

Dread Locks

noun
a hairstyle in which the hair is washed but not combed and twisted while wet into tight braids or ringlets hanging down on all sides

Marcel

noun
a deep artificial wave in the hair, created with a curling iron

Mohawk

noun
a hairstyle in which the head is shaved except for a strip of hair running centrally from the middle of the forehead to the back of the neck

Perm

noun
a hairstyle produced by setting the hair in waves or curls and then treating it with chemicals so that the style lasts for several months

Pigtail

noun
a braid or gathered hank of hair hanging from the back of the head, or either of a pair at the sides

Pixie Cut

noun
a woman's short hairstyle in which the hair is cropped in layers, typically so as to create a slightly tousled effect

Ponytail

noun
a hairstyle in which the hair is drawn back and tied at the back of the head, causing it to hang down like a pony's tail

Shoulder-Length

adj.
(of hair) reaching down to the shoulders

Undercut

noun
a hairstyle in which the hair is shaved or cut very short on the sides or back of the head but left relatively long on top

Updo

noun
a women's hairstyle in which the hair is swept up and fastened away from the face and neck
