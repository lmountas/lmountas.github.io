Clothing Descriptors

A-line

adj.
(of a garment) slightly flared from a narrow waist or shoulders

Asymmetrical

adj.
having parts that fail to correspond to one another in shape, size, or arrangement; lacking symmetry

Avant-Garde

adj.
new and unusual or experimental ideas, especially in the arts, or the people introducing them

Baggy

adj.
(of clothing) loose and hanging in folds:

Boho

adj.
denoting or relating to a woman's fashion aesthetic characterized by flowing print fabrics, layers of clothing, and costume jewelry such as long strings of beads, dangling earrings, and multiple bangles:

Boot-cut

adj.
(of jeans or other trousers) flared very slightly below the knee, so as to be worn comfortably over boots.

Casual

adj.
without formality of style or manner, in particular (of clothing) suitable for everyday wear rather than formal occasions

Cheap

adj.
inexpensive because of inferior quality

Clash

adj.
a mismatch of colors

Clinging

adj.
fitting closely to the body and showing its shape:

Contemporary

adj.
following modern ideas or fashion in style or design

Couture

noun
the design and manufacture of fashionable clothes to a client's specific requirements and measurements fashionable made-to-measure clothes

Crew Neck

noun
a close-fitting round neckline on a sweater or T-shirt

Crisp

adj.
smoothly and attractively stiff and uncreased

Diaphanous

adj.
(especially of fabric) light, delicate, and translucent

Embroidered

adj.
(of a design) sewn on cloth with thread:

Ergonomic

adj.
relating to or designed for efficiency and comfort in the working environment

Evening Wear

noun
clothing, especially for women, that is suitable for formal social occasions

Feminine


having qualities or an appearance traditionally associated with women

Gaudy

adj.
extravagantly bright or showy, typically so as to be tasteless

Masculine

adj.
having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with men

Polished

adj.
refined, sophisticated, or elegant

Printed

adj.
a piece of fabric or clothing with a decorative colored pattern or design printed on it:

Punk

noun
an admirer or player of punk rock, typically characterized by colored spiked hair and clothing decorated with safety pins or zippers.

Retro

adj.
imitative of a style, fashion, or design from the recent past

Ribbed

adj.
(especially of a fabric or garment) having a pattern of raised bands

Ruch

noun
a frill or pleat of fabric as decoration on a garment or home furnishing.

Skin-Tight

adj.
(of a garment) very close-fitting: a skintight black sweater.

Timeless

adj.
not affected by the passage of time or changes in fashion

V-Neck

noun
a neckline of a garment, having straight sides meeting at a point to form a V-shape:

Whimsical

adj.
playfully quaint or fanciful, especially in an appealing and amusing way
